Empowering Seniors with Cybersecurity

Protecting Their Peace of Mind

What YOU can do!

  • Ensure all your devices are up to date
  • Passwords should include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Ideally, passwords for different sites should be unique
  • DO NOT click on suspicious links, which can expose your personal information to hackers
  • Be attentive! If a popup claims your device is compromised, do not click on it
  • Do not make payments using unsecured or public networks
