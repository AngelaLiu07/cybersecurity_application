What are the Types of Cybersecurity?

Malware

  • Code designed to gain access to personal information

Viruses

  • A type of malware that corrupts your device

Ransomware

  • A form of malware that demands money to remove a virus from your device

Phishing

  • Attempts to get sensitive information such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details

Spam Messages or Calls

  • Messages or calls sent out in bulk with harmful intent
